# Quick Steps: Make Repository Private

## 🔒 In Just 5 Steps:

### Step 1: Go to Settings
Navigate to: https://github.com/IskolarJoeySaberon/WriteWise/settings

### Step 2: Scroll to "Danger Zone"
At the bottom of the Settings page

### Step 3: Click "Change visibility"
Found in the "Change repository visibility" section

### Step 4: Select "Private"
Choose the Private option in the dialog

### Step 5: Confirm
Type: `IskolarJoeySaberon/WriteWise` and click confirm

---

## ✅ Done!
Your repository is now private and only accessible to you and your collaborators.

For more detailed information, see: [HOW_TO_MAKE_REPOSITORY_PRIVATE.md](HOW_TO_MAKE_REPOSITORY_PRIVATE.md)
