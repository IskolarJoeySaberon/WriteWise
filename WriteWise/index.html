What is Research?

  • Definition: A systematic process of collecting and analyzing data to increase knowledge.
  • Purpose: Solve problems, build theories, or improve understanding of a topic.
  • Types: Qualitative, Quantitative, or Mixed Methods research.
  • Importance: Drives innovation, policy, education, and informed decision-making.
  • Basic Flow: Topic selection → Literature Review → Methodology → Data Collection → Conclusion.
